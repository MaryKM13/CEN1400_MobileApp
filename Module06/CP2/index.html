What Does Mary Like?

Reading

Mary likes to spend her free time reading, generally science fiction and mysteries. Luckily she can download books from the library on her eReader.

Movies

She also likes to stay up far too late watching movies, everything from cheesy SyFy originals to all of the Marvel movies (except she still has to watch Doctor Strange).

Music

Mary also likes to listen to music when she drives. Her musical likes are all over the map, but her daughter and husband like to listen to the 80's station on SiriusXM.
